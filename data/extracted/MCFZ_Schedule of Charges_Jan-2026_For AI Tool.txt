Introduction
This document outlines the schedule of fees that are currently applicable for the services offered by Masdar City Free Zone.
All the fees are in UAE Dirham.
Payment Methods
Online Payments
Online credit card payment is only available to those applicants who have submitted applications through the Masdar City Free Zone Online Portal or an official payment link.
Credit Card
An applicant can visit our offices and pay using their debit / credit card. Currently, only Visa and Mastercard are accepted.
Bank Transfer
The applicant must inform their point of contact in the Free Zone of their intention to make payment via bank transfer for the specified service.
Once the transaction has been completed, the applicant must forward proof of transfer to their point of contact in the Free Zone that this transfer has been successful, and if possible, provide a copy of the receipt of transfer.
